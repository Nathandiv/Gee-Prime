Bio

Gee Prime's musical journey is marked by a series of innovative singles. His debut track, "A re Deitane," set the tone for his experimental sound. He further showcased his versatility with "Tsa Ko Spain," a project that melds Spanish guitar with Amapiano rhythms, reflecting his deep dive into diverse musical cultures.

Another notable release, "A Matter of Time," pays homage to his appreciation for deep house music. In December 2024, he dropped the single "Lay Low," with a full EP released on February 7, 2025. These works highlight his commitment to pushing musical boundaries and delivering fresh, engaging content to his audience.

Music Hub

Gee Prime's musical repertoire encompasses R&B, Soul, Hip-Hop, Gospel, and House, with each genre reflecting his current emotions.
